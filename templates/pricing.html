Pricing FAQs

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any payments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and debit cards. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Our Free plan lets you experience Post This Today without any commitment. When you're ready for more features, upgrade to Pro with confidence.

What happens if I exceed my post limit?

On the Free plan, you'll need to wait until the next month or upgrade to continue posting. Pro and Enterprise plans have no limits on post creation.
